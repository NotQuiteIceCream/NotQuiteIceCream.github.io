    About Yumini

    Yumini are mini cones topped with a bliss ball enrobed in chocolate and decorated sparkles .  They are hand- made with all natural quality ingredients, with no artificial flavours and no artifical colours*.   Yumini are filled with 100% love and fun ingredients. You can also recreate these Yumini at home using a kit. No special equipment needed.  Keep reading for more on Yumini Kit.                                                       
    The first Yumini was created for Shirley's daughter 7th birthday party.  It was topped with Rocky Road and ice cream. After attending a family birthday party, it was obvious Yumini need a topping that can be enjoyed by everyone. So the Cocobliss Yumini was created.

    Yumini can be stored in room temperature. No refrigeration is needed which make them ideal in outdoor events or functions. As long as they are stored in cool dry places and away from direct sunlight in air tight containers, they will last for at least one month (that is if they are not eaten first!). Refrigeration is not recommended the cool temperature may cause the chocolate to ' bloom' - a white colour layer on top of the chocolate.  Blooming does not affect the quality of chocolate, just the appearance. 

    Yumini are great for birthday parties, weddings, functions, baby showers and girls night-in.   The colourful 'treasure box' and fun element makes them an attractive item for your organisation's fund raising event. Who can resist Yumini's charm?                             

    *except on certain designs. Contact us for details.

    Our Ingredients

    Shirley only uses ingredients that she feels comfortable for her own children. All ingredients are carefully selected to include good nutrition without sacrificing the fun factor. 

    There are no artificial flavours or colours in any of the Yumini*.   They are preservative and sulphite free. 


    Cocobliss Yumini contains:

    Organic dessciated coconut, Australian sultana and currants, Australia popped corn, Australia full cream milk powder, Organic pure raw cocoa butter, Organic honey, White chocolate, Dark chocolate, Mini ice crea cones.                                                  

    Yumini DIY Kit

    You or your little ones can amaze your family and friends with this Yumini DIY kit. It contains all that you need for making these Yumini at home either for yourself or as a gift.  Best thing is no fancy kitchen gadgets are needed.  The Cocobliss balls comes as a log. All you need is to cut them into portions. Melt the chocolate and you are on your way to create Yumini yourself.  You can even try tempering some of the chocoalte for the chocoltier's finish.

    Perfect for children party activities or a rainy day activities They also make a yummy educational tool for primary schoolers and OOSH. Children will learn about temperature, different states of matter (solid melts into a liquid and into solids again with cooling),   sustability, and geometry as they will need to make paper cones.  Cocobliss Yumini does not contain any nuts in the ingredients. *

     Adults equally loves making them. Use them next time in your team building activity in the office. Maybe form teams and have a competition for the best design!

    Each Yumini DIY Kit makes 6 Yuminis. The kit contains:
    - all ingredients, including 6 mini cones and decoration sprinkles                                                               
     - cups for dipping and coating 
     - Indvidual cone stands

    FAQ

    1. Is Yumini Australian made?
    Yes. Yumini and the DIY kits are made in the Hills District in Sydney, Australia using local and imported ingredients. Shirley sources Australian ingredients whenever possible.

    2. Where can I buy Yumini or the DIY kit
    You can order through ebay at the moment until the Retail function is set up on the website. You can also order through Facebook or email notquiteicecream@gmail.com

    3. Do you send Australia wide?
    Yes - the DIY kit can be shipped Australia wide. It is shipped in a tamper proof container that will protect the contents.

    4. Do you have gluten free Yumini?
    Yes - there are also gluten free Yumini avaialble.  Please contact Shirley first as the cones are larger than the wheat/gluten containing cones. 

    5. Are Yumini nut free?
    Nuts are not in cocobliss Yumini as an ingredients. However, as the ingredients that make Yumini may contain traces of nuts or tree nuts, Yumini cannot claim that it is totally nut free. 

    6. Does Yumini has any artificial colour, flavours or preservatives?
    No -Cocobliis Yumini does not contain any artificial colour, flavours or preservatives. Organic desscicated coconut, instead of regular desscicated coconut, is used so there is no sulphite.
