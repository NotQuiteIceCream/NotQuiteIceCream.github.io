About Yumini

Yumini are mini cones topped with a chocolate coated bliss ball that are made to look like an ice-cream.  They are hand- made with all natural quality ingredients, with no artificial flavours, no artificial colours* and no preservatives.   Yumini are filled with 100% love and fun. 

You can recreate these Yumini at home using a DIY kit.  No special equipment is needed.                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           
The first Yumini was topped with Rocky Road. After attending a family birthday party, it was obvious Yumini needed a topping that can be enjoyed by all generations. So Yumini topped with a 'cocobliss ball' was created. Yumini are great for birthday parties, weddings, functions, baby showers and girls night-in.   

How to make Yumini???

Melt the chocolates

Either using hot water bath method (like above) or using a microwave. Adult supervision is needed.

Coat the rim of each cone with some chocolate

Arrange the cone holders. Dip the rim of each cone in the melted white chocolate. Twist the cone to coat a good amount of white chocolate onto the rim. Then place the cone in one cone holder.  

Attach

Roll the bliss ball a little. You may also want to leave it in the freezer for a few minutes. You can do this while you are melting the chocolate. Wait for a minute or so to let the white chocolate set hard - this glues the ball onto the cone.

Coat

Holding the cone, gently and slowly lower the ball into the melted dark chocolate and coat it entirely. 

Decorate!

Decorate with sprinkles provided or other sprinkles you have!

Add your own touch!! 

Why not drizzle any remaining white chocolate to create these looks! Arrange the sprinkles on the chocolate drizzle to create another fun designs. 

Yumini DIY Kit

The DIY kit contains all that material needed for making these Yuminis. Basic crockery is all that needed. No mixing required. 

Each kits contains:

- bliss ball portions and  waffle cones

- dark chocolate and/or white chocolate pieces

-  cone stands, white paper cups and colour paper cases

- assorted sprinkles. Standard sprinkles are star sprinkles

- Full colour instruction sheet  

(image show a 6 Yuminis DIY kit)                                                                                  

Follow these 4 steps:                            

1) Take the bliss ball portions out of its packaging. Roll and chill in freezer for 5 minutes  

2) Melt two types of chocolates in separate containers using hot tap water

3) Coat the rim with some melted white chocolate and attach the bliss ball on the mini cone  

4) Coat the bliss ball with the melted dark chocolate and decorate.


It only takes  15 minutes from opening the kit to finish.  And the result? Irresistible, colourful delicious Yuminis.

What is tempering?

Tempering chocolate is an essential step for making smooth, glossy, evenly coloured coating for your dipped chocolates. If you plan to make Yumini in advance for your event, we suggest that you temper the dark chocolate for dipping. We can provide you with instruction to how to temper chocolate when you order Yumini.

How should I store them?

Store Yumini at room temperature in an airtight container in cool dry places and away from direct sunlight once they are made. Refrigeration is not recommended as the cool temperature may cause the chocolate to ' bloom' - a white colour layer on top of the chocolate. Blooming does not affect the quality of chocolate, just the appearance.
  

How long does it last once I receive them?

Yumini is made with all natural ingredients with no preservatives. 'Best By' date is indicated on the label. For Yumini the date is two months from when they are made to ensure freshness.
