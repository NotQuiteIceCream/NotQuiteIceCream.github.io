    • Our Story

    Not Quite Ice Cream is a local business based in West Pennant Hills. It was started by Shirley Chan who is a mum of two beautiful girls. 

    Like every mum, Shirley knows children want their party food to be fun and colourful.  Shirley made  Yumini (short for 'Yummy Minis' ) for her daughter's birthday party to add the 'wow' factor for her and her little friends.   Yumini was a huge success.  

    It is the Shirley's hope that Yumini can help raise funds for cancer support and other causes. 




    About Yumini

    Yumini are mini cone topped with chocolate cluster and finished off with a chocolate dip and sparkles .  They are hand- made with all natural quality ingredients, with no artificial flavours and no artifical colours*.   Yumini are filled with 100% love and fun ingredients. Scroll down to read more about our ingredients.  Yumini also come in a DIY kit.

    Our first Yumini formula is topped with popcorn Rocky Road. More flavours are coming. 

    Yuminies can be stored in room temperature. No refrigeration is needed which make them ideal in outdoor parties or functions. As long as they are stored in cool dry places and away from direct sunlight in air tight containers, they will last for at least one month (that is if they are not eaten first!). Refrigeration is not recommended the cool temperature may cause the chocolate to ' bloom' - a white colour layer on top of the chocolate.  Blooming does not affect the quality of chocolate, just the appearance. 

    Yuminies are great for birthday parties, weddings, functions, baby showers and girls night-in.   

    *except on certain designs. Contact us for details.

    Our Ingredients

    Shirley only uses ingredients that she feels comfortable for her own children. All ingredients are carefully selected to include good nutrition without sacrificing the fun factor. 

    Popcorn Rocky Road Yuminies contains: 
    Milk chocolate, dark chocolate, marshmallow, coconut, sultana and popcorn

    There are no artificial flavours or colours in any of the Yuminies*.   

    Gluten free Yuminies are also available - they are equally delicious. Using her knowledge as a qualified dietitian, Shirley checks every single ingredient to ensure they are gluten free. In addition, Shirley also uses separate utensils and equipment to ensure no cross contamination can occur.

    How to make Yumini

    Making Yumini is simple. No special kitchen gadget is needed. Just some tall glasses, a bowl to fit the glass, a teaspoon, a plate and some hot tap water.


    All you do is:
    Melt and Mix
    Fill the mini cups
    Melt and Dip
    Decorate
