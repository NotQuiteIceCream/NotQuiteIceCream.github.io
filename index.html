    • Our Story

    Not Quite Ice Cream is a local business based in West Pennant Hills. It was started by Shirley Chan who is a mum of two beautiful girls. 

    Like every mum, Shirley knows children want their party food to be fun and colourful.  Shirley made  Yumini (short for 'Yummy Mini' ) for her daughter's birthday party to add the 'wow' factor for her and her little friends.   Yumini was a huge success.  

    Yumini was well received by both adult and children. Shirley hopes that Yumini can help to raise funds for cancer support and other causes.  

    Cones for Causes.

    About Yumini

    Yumini are mini cones topped with a bliss ball enrobed in dark chocolate and decorated with sprinkles.  They are hand- made with all natural quality ingredients, with no artificial flavours, no artifical colours* and no preservatives.   Yumini are filled with 100% love and fun ingredients. You can recreate these Yumini at home using a DIY kit. No special equipments are needed.  Keep reading for more on Yumini DIY Kit.                                  

                                                                                                                                          
    The first Yumini was created for Shirley's daughter 7th birthday party.  It was topped with Rocky Road and ice cream. After attending a family birthday party, it was obvious Yumini need a topping that can be enjoyed by everyone. So Yumini topped with a 'cocobliss ball' was created.

    Yumini are great for birthday parties, weddings, functions, baby showers and girls night-in.   The colourful 'treasure box' and fun element makes them an attractive item for your organisation's fund raising event. Who can resist Yumini's charm?                             

    *except on certain designs. Contact us for details.

    Our Ingredients

    Shirley only uses ingredients that she feels comfortable for her own children. All ingredients are carefully selected to include good nutrition without sacrificing the fun factor. 

    There are no artificial flavours or colours in any of the Yumini*.   They are preservative and sulphite free. 


    Yumini 'cocobliss' contains:

    Organic dessciated coconut, Australian sultana and currants, Australian popped corn, Australian full cream milk powder, organic pure raw cocoa butter, Organic honey, white chocolate, dark chocolate and mini ice crea cones.                                                  

    Yumini DIY Kit

    Yumini DIY kit can help turn your fund raising event into a huge success. The Yumini DIY kit will only takes you 20 minutes to make 6 Yumini and the result? Irresistable, colourful delicious mini cones that will fly off your stall. The DIY kit contains all that you need for making these Yumini.  Best thing is no fancy kitchen gadgets are needed.  All you need is a plate, a bread knife, a couple of spoons and glasses. You don't even need to mix anything. The bliss ball comes in as a log . All you need is to cut it into equal portions, melt the chocolates provided and you are on your way to create Yumini yourself.  You can even try tempering some of the chocolate for the chocolatier's finish.

    Perfect for children party or as a wedding favour. They are easy to personalised and your guest will be intrigued. 

    Yumini DIY kit makes a good education tool for pre schools, primary schools and OOSH.  Children will learn about temperature, different states of matter (solid melts into a liquid and into solids again with cooling), sustability, and geometry as they will need to make paper cones.  Cocobliss Yumini does not contain any nuts in the ingredients. *

    Adults equally loves making them. Use Yumini next time in your team building activity in the office. Maybe form teams and have a competition for the best design! 

    Each Yumini DIY Kit makes 6 Yuminis. Each kit contains:
    - all ingredients, including 6 mini cones and decoration sprinkles          
    - bliss balls comes as a log. You just need to cut and shape.                                                   
     - cups for dipping and coating 
     - indvidual cone stands

    FAQ

    1. Is Yumini Australian made?
    Yes. Yumini and the DIY kits are handmade in the Hills District in Sydney, Australia using local and imported ingredients. Shirley sources Australian ingredients whenever possible.

    2. Where can I buy Yumini or the DIY kit
    You can order through ebay at the moment until the Retail function is set up on our website. You can also order through Facebook or email notquiteicecream@gmail.com

    3. Do you send Australia wide?
    Yes - the DIY kit can be shipped Australia wide. It is shipped in a tamper proof plastic container that will protect the contents. The plastic container is fully recyclable (plastic number 5).

    4. Do you have gluten free Yumini?
    Yes - there are also gluten free Yumini avaialble.  Please contact Shirley first as the cones are larger than the wheat/gluten containing cones. 

    5. Are Yumini nut free?
    Nuts are not in cocobliss Yumini as an ingredient. However, as they are home made using ingredients that make Yumini may contain traces of nuts or tree nuts, Yumini is not 100% totally nut free. 

    6. Does Yumini has any artificial colour, flavours or preservatives?
    No -Cocobliis Yumini does not contain any artificial colour, flavours or preservatives. Organic desscicated coconut, instead of regular desscicated coconut, is used so there is no sulphite. 

    7. What are the charities or causes that you have in mind?
    Shirley has her local primary school in mind to start for their fund raising effort. Other organisations  including Breast Cancer Network Australia, Canteen and Save the Children are other charities Shirley and her husband are personally supporting. We hope that Yumini can contribute to these charities. 

    With your support, these little cones could truly become Cones for Causes. If you have a cause that you are passionate about, please contact us to discuss how Yumini can help your cause. 

    8. How should I store them?
    If the force is with you and your will power can stop yourself from eating them all once you received or made them, we suggest that you store Yumini at room temperature in an airtight container in cool dry places and away from direct sunlight. Refrigeration is not recommended the cool temperature may cause the chocolate to ' bloom' - a white colour layer on top of the chocolate. Blooming does not affect the quality of chocolate, just the appearance.  

    9. How long does it last once I receive them?
    Yumini is made with all natural ingredients with no preservatives. 'Best By' date is indicated on the label. For Yumini the date is two months from when they are made to ensure they are at the freshest. 

    10. Do you have any other topping or flavours?
    We are always trying to think of new topping and flavours. Please send us your idea for new topping and decoration ideas. 

    11. Do you have an ABN?
    Not Quite Ice Cream ABN 41 009 192 602 

    12. So as a dietitian, you give your children chocolate?
    Of course! Kids are kids!
