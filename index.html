    • Our Story

    Not Quite Ice Cream is a local business based in West Pennant Hills. It was started by Shirley Chan who is a mum of two beautiful girls. 

    Like every mum, Shirley knows children want their party food to be fun and colourful.  Shirley made  Yumini (short for 'Yummy Minis' ) for her daughter's birthday party to add the 'wow' factor for her and her little friends.   Yumini was a huge success.  

    It is the Shirley's hope that Yumini can help raise funds for cancer support and other causes.

    About Yumini

    Yumini are mini cone topped with chocolate cluster and finished off with a chocolate dip and sparkles .  They are hand- made with all natural quality ingredients, with no artificial flavours and no artifical colours*.   Yumini are filled with 100% love and fun ingredients. You can also recreate these Yumini at home using a kit. No special equipment needed.  Keep reading for more on Yumini Kit.                           

    Our first Yumini is topped with popcorn Rocky Road and enrobed in dark chocolate. Perfectly balanced with soft chewiness, some crunch and a little sweetness. More flavours are coming. 

    Yuminies can be stored in room temperature. No refrigeration is needed which make them ideal in outdoor parties or functions. As long as they are stored in cool dry places and away from direct sunlight in air tight containers, they will last for at least one month (that is if they are not eaten first!). Refrigeration is not recommended the cool temperature may cause the chocolate to ' bloom' - a white colour layer on top of the chocolate.  Blooming does not affect the quality of chocolate, just the appearance. 

    Yuminies are great for birthday parties, weddings, functions, baby showers and girls night-in.   

    *except on certain designs. Contact us for details.

    Our Ingredients

    Shirley only uses ingredients that she feels comfortable for her own children. All ingredients are carefully selected to include good nutrition without sacrificing the fun factor. 

    Popcorn Rocky Road Yuminies contains: 
    Milk chocolate, dark chocolate, marshmallow, coconut, sultana, popcorn and mini cone of course!

    There are no artificial flavours or colours in any of the Yuminies*.   

    Gluten free Yuminies are also available - they are equally delicious. Using her knowledge as a qualified dietitian, Shirley checks every single ingredient to ensure they are gluten free. In addition, Shirley also uses separate utensils and equipment to ensure no cross contamination can occur.                       

    Our packaging

    All the packaging used in Yumini Kit are made from plant, not plastic.


    The brown paper carton uses paper and card from recycled or sustainable sources

    The clear bags used are a type of clear home-compostable film made from sustainably-sourced wood pulp. It has certification for Chain of Custody from FSC & PEFC.

    The small clear cups are plant-based PLA, contains 79% less embodied carbon than plastic

    All materials are BPA-free, compostible and biodegradable. 

    Go to http://www.vegware.com.au for more information.

    Yumini DIY Kit

    You or your little ones can amaze your family and friends with this Yumini DIY kit. It contains all that you need for making these Yumini at home either for yourself or as a gift.  Best thing is no fancy kitchen gadgets are needed. Just a spoon, a couple of glasses, a bowl, a plate and a fridge. You can even try tempering some of the chocoalte for the chocoltier's finish.

    Perfect for children party activities or a rainy day activities They also make a yummy educational tool for primary schoolers and OOSH. Children will learn about temperature, different states of matter (solid melts into a liquid and into solids again with cooling),  mixture, sustability, and geometry as they will need to make paper cones.  Popcorn Roacky Road Yumini does not contain any nuts in the ingredients. *

    Yumini is not only for children. Adults equally loves making them. Use them next time in your team building activity in the office. Maybe form teams and have a competition for the best design!

    Each Yumini DIY Kit makes 6 Yuminis. The kit contains:
    - all ingredients, including 6 mini cones and decoration sprinkles

    Yes. Yumini and the DIY kits are made in the Hills District in Sydney, Australia using local and imported ingredients. Shirley sources Australian ingredients whenever possible.

    3. Do you send Australia wide?

    4. Do you have gluten free Yumini?
    Yes - there are also gluten free Yumini avaialble. The cones are larger than the gluten containing counterpart.
